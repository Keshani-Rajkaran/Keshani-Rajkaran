PROJECT 01

ERP Scheduling System for Mckaylan Motor Engineering C.C

The Business Problem:
The company relied on manual paper diaries and fragmented spreadsheets, leading to double-bookings and lost revenue. Customers were required to physically visit the workshop for appointments, causing friction and limiting growth.

Key Complexities & Engineered Solutions
  • ‚óª Admin Operations Console: I designed a full administrative control panel that allows authorized users to create, edit, and delete appointments, manage customer records, and access historical report data. This ensures staff can correct errors and maintain accuracy without developer intervention.
  • ‚óª Managerial Decision Dashboard: I engineered a separate Manager Dashboard that transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights. Managers can generate performance and seasonal reports to make evidence-based decisions rather than relying on guesswork.
  • ‚óª Staff & Resource Allocation Engine: Managers can add technicians, edit staff records, and assign technicians to specific appointments. This ensures the right staff are deployed to the right jobs at the right time.
  • ‚óª Customer Management System (CRM): Includes a module that allows admins to add, edit, and remove customer records. This enables the business to track customer history and support repeat business.
Succesful Outcomes

By separating the system into Customer, Admin, and Manager environments, the platform mirrors how real businesses operate. Customers can self-book, administrators can control operations, and managers can run the business strategically using data rather than intuition. This implementation achieved 100% elimination of double-bookings.
