Project 1: ERP Scheduling System for McKaylan Motor Engineering C.C.

Business Problem: Fragmented paper diaries and manual spreadsheets led to chronic double-bookings and revenue leakage.

Engineered Solution: Built a full ERP platform that replaced paper-based operations with a secure digital system, enabling online customer self-service and real-time technician scheduling.

Security Architecture

  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Hierarchical separation of Admin, Manager, and Customer privileges.
  • Encrypted Authentication: Multi-layer session protection to safeguard private dashboards.
  • Validation Logic: Server-side sanitization and duplicate detection.
  • Access Blocked Protocols: Automatic session termination for unauthorized activity.
  • Temporal Rules: Logic protecting historical record integrity while allowing future bookings.

Business Impact

Achieved 100% elimination of double-bookings and enabled a ~40% increase in customer acquisition through digital self-service.
