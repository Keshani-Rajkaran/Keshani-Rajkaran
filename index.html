02. BI Seasonal Analytics Engine

For the Enterprise Seasonal Analytics system, I designed and implemented a logic-driven Business Intelligence engine using C# and ASP.NET to transform historical transactional records into performing executive insights. I implemented complex data aggregation modules that generate Daily Service Demand Trends and Workforce Efficiency Analytics, allowing management to stress-test technician loads and track throughput rates across specific business cycles. A core innovation of this project was the development of the Strategic Reccomendations system, which utilizes logic-driven analysis of client acquisition and retention ratios to automate high-level business recommendations, such as referral incentives or proactive workforce scaling. By digitizing these analytical workflows into professional, print-ready reports, the system provides management with Data-Driven Strategic Intuition, enabling them to align staffing and growth strategies with forecasted seasonal peaks three months in advance.
