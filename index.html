PROJECT 01

Enterprise ERP & Scheduling System

Client: McKaylan Motor Engineering C.C
Context: Automotive engineering & service operations

The Business Problem:
The company relied on paper diaries, phone calls, and in-person visits to manage customer bookings. Customers had to physically go to the workshop just to request an appointment. This caused friction, limited after-hours enquiries, double-bookings, lost jobs, and serious revenue leakage.

Digital Transformation Solution
  • Customer Self-Service Portal: Customers create accounts, request bookings from home, and view appointment confirmations in real-time. This turned the business into a 24/7 digital storefront and attracted new customers.
  • Admin Operations Console: Admin users can create, edit and delete appointments, manage customer records, and access report history to maintain operational accuracy.
  • Manager Intelligence Dashboard: Managers generate staff, seasonal and revenue reports, assign technicians to jobs, and optimize workforce utilization.
  • Staff & Job Allocation Engine: Technicians are assigned to appointments based on workload and availability.
Core Engineering Challenges Solved
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Secure separation of Customer, Admin, and Manager privileges.
  • Centralized SQL Data Model: Eliminates double-bookings and ensures system-wide data consistency.
  • Business Intelligence Layer: Converts raw operational data into actionable management insights.
  • Process Automation: Manual paper workflows replaced with validated digital transactions.
Security & Data Integrity
  • Encrypted authentication & session management
  • Server-side input validation
  • Unauthorized access detection
  • Temporal logic prevents historical data manipulation
Business Impact

The system achieved 100% elimination of double-bookings, enabled remote customer onboarding, captured every billable job, and gave management real-time visibility into staff performance and revenue.
