Project 1: ERP Scheduling System - McKaylan Motor Engineering

Business Problem: Manual paper diaries led to chronic double-bookings and lost revenue visibility.

Solution: For the McKaylan Motor Engineering ERP, I designed and implemented a multi-tier scheduling ecosystem that replaced fragmented manual diaries with a secure, automated digital hub. I implemented a Customer Self-Service Portal for remote bookings directly from the businesses website, an Admin Operations dashboard for booking and management of appointments, full lifecycle management of customer records, and a Managerial Decision Dashboard that transforms raw transactional data into actionable operational insights through a reporting system, and a system that allows managers to manage technicians and other staff. The system features a robust Staff & Resource Allocation Engine to manage technician workloads and a Lightweight CRM to track customer history and improve retention. Architecturally, I enforced high-level security through Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), encrypted authentication, and temporal logic to protect the integrity of historical data, ultimately achieving 100% elimination of double-bookings.
