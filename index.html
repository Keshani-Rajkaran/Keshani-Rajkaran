PROJECT 1

ERP Scheduling System for Mckaylan Motor Engineering C.C

The Business Problem: McKaylan Motor Engineering relied on manual paper diaries and fragmented spreadsheets, leading to scheduling conflicts and significant revenue leakage from unrecorded billable hours.The firm relied on in-person and phone-based bookings, forcing customers to physically visit the premises to request appointments. This created friction, limited after-hours enquiries, and caused the business to lose potential customers who were not willing or able to come in just to schedule a service. In addition, bookings recorded on paper diaries were prone to errors, overlaps, and revenue leakage.
